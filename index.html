About us

What does JIHClubs do?

JIHClubs is a community helping students find new hobbies within college. And also increase the interest of students in clubs. Or help them create their own.

How does this help students?

Our community helps students take their minds off lessons in this way. Give students new interesting skills. Or develop those that already exist.

Why do clubs need ratings?

Ratings are needed to improve the performance of clubs. Or as a motivation for those responsible for the club to work further. This is a kind of fitback.

Can I create my own club?

Yes, anyone can create their own club. If you have any skills or knowledge that you could share.

What do you need to create your own club?

There are several recommendations that you will need to follow. First, make and defend a presentation about your club in front of JIHClubs. This is necessary so that we can know what your club is about. Secondly, have a ready-made plan for the year. Thirdly, make several advertising posters.
